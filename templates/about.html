About Jan Seva Bank

Our Mission

At Jan Seva Bank, our mission is to provide accessible, secure, and innovative banking solutions to empower individuals and businesses across India. We believe in financial inclusion and strive to make banking services available to everyone.

Our History

Established in 1985, Jan Seva Bank has been serving customers for over three decades. What started as a small community bank has now grown into a trusted financial institution with millions of customers across the country.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We conduct our business with the highest ethical standards.
  • Innovation: We continuously evolve to meet the changing needs of our customers.
  • Inclusion: We believe banking should be accessible to all.
  • Service: We put our customers at the center of everything we do.
